Contact

Koberl at Blue is located at 998 Monterey Street in the historic J.P. Andrews Building, circa 1894 at the corner of Monterey and Osos Street in Downtown San Luis Obispo.

We are open for indoor service with 50% of capacity as well as outdoor sidewalk dining, take-out and curbside pick-up. Our opening times are Tuesdays through Saturdays from 4:00pm to 9:00pm.
