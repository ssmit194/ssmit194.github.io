About

Enjoy a glass of wine or your favorite cocktail with dinner in our gracious dining room or while relaxing with family and friends in our urban bar. We would be delighted to be the place where you choose to unwind from a busy day or a stressful week or to celebrate a birthday, anniversary, a good day or the end of a bad day.

We are open Tuesday through Saturday from 4:00pm to 9:00pm. We are CLOSED on Sunday and Monday and the following holidays: New Years Day, Memorial Day, 4th of July, Labor Day, Thanksgiving Day & Christmas Day. Due to limited seating we suggest reservations. Thank you!
