Introduction

Nikonos Project #5

Nikonos Project #5 is a magazine based around photographs and stories made with the Nikonos V camera. The camera was built as a film diving camera. Because it was enginered to survive trips underwater, it is extremly strong and can withstand any adventure. Many people have taken amazing picutres with this camera in wild places and it continues to tell the story even after 30 years underwater.
